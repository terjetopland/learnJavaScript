As a computer science student at my last year I saw that I needed to get "my hands dirty" outside the classroom.
After listen to freecodecamps podcast, there was this guy who mentioned to learning in public. By this he ment to just go out there and start coding with a public repo that evolves as you go. In that way you can get feedback along the way if others see something that is not correct - and you can refactor and change along the way.
